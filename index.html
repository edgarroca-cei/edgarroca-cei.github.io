About The Game

Flesh Fest is a survival horror action-adventure game focused on exploration, stealth, and puzzles.

You play as Ruby, trapped within a mysterious castle. Overcome puzzles, traps, and monsters including Murray, a grotesque creature hunting you relentlessly. Face challenges in your desperate fight to escape.

  • Explore: Discover a twisted map in this 2.5D adventure. Wander through castle towers, toxic gardens, and hidden laboratories.
  • Solve: Untangle puzzles and riddles that open new paths and uncover the story within the castle.
  • Hide: Use stealth to avoid bizarre creatures roaming the castle's corridors.
  • Fight: Use tools, weapons and magical runes to your advantage.

Atmosphere

A mysterious adventure through a surreal world of monsters and magic. Inspired by Little Nightmares, Hollow Knight, Ghibli films, and Steven Universe.

Flesh Fest features hand-drawn animations and 2.5D backgrounds for enhanced immersion.
